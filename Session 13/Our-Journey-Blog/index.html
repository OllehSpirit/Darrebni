S O U V E N I R

Take something home to your loved ones without paying extra baggage fees.

Postcards

Postcards are cheap, easy to find and take up virtually no space. Look for postcards that reflect something specific that you love about the place you’re visiting.

Recipes

If we could make the food we’ve tried while traveling last forever, we’d never need another souvenir again.

Photos

photos are a very real way to remember exactly how something looked, Limit yourself to just one or two pictures. You’ll find that the photos you do take are more inspired and well thought out.

New Facebook Friends

Meeting new people can completely define your trip. Connect with your newfound friends on social media. When you see their names in your newsfeed, you’ll have an instant burst of memory about the times you shared while traveling.
